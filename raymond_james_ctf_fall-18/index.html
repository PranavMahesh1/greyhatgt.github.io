Raymond James CTF FY'18

As announced last Thursday, our CTF leadership was invited to send a team to this year’s Raymond James CTF and decided to use IceCTF 2018 as a qualifier. The top individual scorers from IceCTF (see CTFtime) were selected to represent Georgia Tech at this year’s Raymond James CTF. They are: Stephen Tong of “perfect blue“, Neil Thistlethwaite of “doge,” William Guilian of “pear0,” Oswin So of “.,” and Jerry Huang of “sice.”
